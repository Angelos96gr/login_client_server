MucDocs is a platform for patients to find doctors in the area of Munich. Additionally, doctors can register their practise so that the patient can contact them to book appointments.

The web app supports:
  • Login/out of the webpage
  • Creating user accounts with credentials and passwords
  • All sensitive information such as passwords are stored hashed in the database
  • Registering the practise as a doctor
  • Accessing a list of registered doctors in the area
